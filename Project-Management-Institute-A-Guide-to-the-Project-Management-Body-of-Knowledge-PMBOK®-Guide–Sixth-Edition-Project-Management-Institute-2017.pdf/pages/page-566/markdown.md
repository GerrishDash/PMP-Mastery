### 3.21.1 PROJECT MANAGEMENT PLAN COMPONENTS

Examples of project management plan components that may be inputs for this process include but are not limited to:

- ◆ Risk management plan,
- ◆ Scope baseline,
- ◆ Schedule baseline, and
- ◆ Cost baseline.
